Autonomous Delivery. The future of Shopping!

What is Autonomous Delivery

In our current age people deliver your packages, food, goods, etc. But in the future these hard, complicated and unfulfilling jobs won't be needed. We will have robots and drones to do all of these jobs. This will also make the object you're ordering less expensive since the companies don't need to pay drivers.


Will this benefit society?

This is not anything new. During Covid-19 many people used robots to get their deliveries since many delivery men were deemed as "non-essential" workers.
